Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how West Wickham Underwater Hockey Club ("WWUWH", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Wickham Wave mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account (name, email address)
  • Update your profile
  • RSVP to events
  • Make payments for sessions or memberships
  • Enable push notifications

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send you notifications about events you're invited to
  • Track attendance for club sessions
  • Manage your membership and subscription
  • Respond to your comments and questions

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With other club members: Your name and attendance status may be visible to other members for event coordination
  • Payment processors: We use Stripe to process payments securely
  • Service providers: We use Cloudflare and Supabase to host and operate our Service

Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, we collect your device token to send you notifications about event invitations. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption for all data transmission and secure storage of your data.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of push notifications

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Website: wwuwh.com
  • Through the club's official communication channels
