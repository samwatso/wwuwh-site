Play abroad. Come back sharper.

Tournaments

West Wickham takes pride in organising and travelling to tournaments across the UK and Europe. These weekends are where fitness meets finesse, higher-tempo games, different tactics, new pools and new playing styles and where the pressure turns training into instinct. Just as importantly, tournaments are a chance to experience the international underwater hockey community: welcoming, diverse, brilliantly competitive, and often where the strongest friendships are made between games.

Different pools. Same puck. Same team.

  • Sharpen skills against unfamiliar playing styles
  • Build team chemistry through intense match blocks
  • Represent the club and bring the energy home
  • Discover new cities, new clubs, new friends
