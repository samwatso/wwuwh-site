Delete Your Account

You can delete your Wickham Wave account at any time. When you delete your account, all of your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems.

How to Delete Your Account

In the Mobile App:

  1. Open the Wickham Wave app
  2. Tap on Profile in the bottom navigation
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the Profile page
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

On the Web:

  1. Go to wwuwh.com/app/profile
  2. Sign in to your account
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
  4. Click Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted
What gets deleted:
  • Your account and profile information
  • Your event RSVP history
  • Your push notification settings
  • Your membership records

Need Help?

If you're having trouble deleting your account or have any questions, please contact us through the club's official communication channels or visit wwuwh.com.
