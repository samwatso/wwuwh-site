The route to your first session

Contact, kit, then get in the water.

Underwater hockey is perfect for swimmers, freedivers, finswimmers and anyone who loves being in the water. We plan sessions around numbers and ability (drills, match play, team practice), so please message us before you book or turn up so we can slot you in safely and make your first session great.

Connect first

Drop us a message before you book. We'll confirm the best night, what to bring, and whether the session is drills, match play, or team practice.

Regularly checking WhatsApp and Instagram to make your first session better.

Message us

Get the right kit

Mask, snorkel and fins are the basics. If you're new, we can often help with club kit for your first few sessions.

View Kit Guide

Register + turn up

Once we've confirmed the best session for you, sign up to our members app to RSVP for sessions and keep track of what's coming up. On the night we'll introduce you, pair you with someone, and ease you into drills or game time based on experience.
